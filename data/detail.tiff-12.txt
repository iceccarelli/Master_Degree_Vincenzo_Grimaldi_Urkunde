5.2 Access to a regulated profession

According to the provisions of the NRW Engineer's Act, specifically pursuant to Section 1 Para. 1
No. 1 Letter a in the currently valid edition of the Law for the Protection of the Occupational Title
“Ingenieur/Ingenieurin" of May 5, 1970 (GV. NRW. S. 312), successful completion of the course of
study allows the graduate to use the professional title "Ingenieur" or "Ingenieurin" (Engineer).
